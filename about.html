About Us

Our Story

A Culinary Journey

Founded in 2010 by Chef Michael Laurent, Saveur began as a small bistro with a big dream: to create a dining experience that celebrates the art of flavor and the joy of gathering around a table.

Our journey started in a modest space with just ten tables, but our passion for exceptional cuisine quickly earned us a devoted following. As word spread about our innovative dishes and warm hospitality, we expanded to our current location in the heart of the city.

Over the years, we've remained true to our founding philosophy: source the finest seasonal ingredients, prepare them with respect and creativity, and serve them in an atmosphere of genuine hospitality. Every dish tells a story of tradition, innovation, and the diverse culinary influences that inspire our team.

Today, Saveur is proud to be recognized as one of the city's premier dining destinations, but we still approach each day with the same enthusiasm and attention to detail that defined us from the beginning. We invite you to join us and become part of our continuing story.
